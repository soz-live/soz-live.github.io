## Introduction

**SOZ-LIVE** is not just a code base to be downloaded and used. It is also a community of users and coders that come together to further develop and improve the project.  

All members actions are governed by the [Code of Conduct](https://github.com//soz-live//soz-releases//blob//main//CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md).


## Aims and Objectives

The objectives of the **SOZ-LIVE** community are to:

- maintain the safety and integrity of the project.
- share ideas and improve the project.
- further the development of the project.
- introduce the project to a wider audience.

While aiming to be:

- fair
- respectful
- inclusive
- supportive
- welcoming
- open
- diverse
- caring
- sharing
